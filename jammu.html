Jannat-e-Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is home to several valleys such as the Kashmir Valley, Chenab Valley, Sindh Valley and Lidder Valley. Some major tourist attractions in Jammu and Kashmir are Srinagar, with its renowned
Dal Lake and Mughal Gardens, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Bhaderwah, Patnitop and Jammu. Jammu and Kashmir is well-known for its snow-covered mountains, famous mosques, and local craftwork.
The gardens and lakes of the Kashmir valley attract many tourists from all over the world. Besides being acclaimed for its scenic beauty and hospitality of people, tourism in J&K has received remarkable impetus
for its distinctive features like diverse physical terrain, pleasant climate, exotic cuisine, distinct socio-cultural ethos, archeological sites, magnificent lakes, ideal spots for adventure sports.Famous for
Pashmina shawls, carved walnut woodwork, papier machie, and more across the world, Jammu is indeed a hevean for Shopaholics on earth.

Food & Culture

Both Jammu and Kashmir regions have delicious dishes on offer, however, some of them have become quite popular even for the tourists. The region of Kashmir, in fact, with its variety of dishes on offer, has become a hub for culinary tourism. Here are the top dishes from Jammu & Kashmir to try on your holiday such as :
1. Dumaaloo
2. Chaaman
3. Rogan Josh
4. Rajma Chawal
5. Kalaadi Kulchaa
